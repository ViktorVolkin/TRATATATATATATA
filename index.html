contactsSABATONTour Dates & Tickets | Sabaton Official Website Sabaton is a Swedish heavy metal band formed in December 1999. The main theme for the songs are wars, individual battles and feats. The name of the group in English means a plate shoe, a part of the knight's armor.
Some of their most popular songs are: Bismarck,Shiroyama,Primo Victoria,The Last Stand. Quote: "Most of the songs of sabaton are very powerful, they increase the strength of mind and motivation, because of these qualities are well suited for training."
